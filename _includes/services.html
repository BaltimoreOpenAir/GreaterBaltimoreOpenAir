WeatherCubes

The Box

Designed by Yan Azdoud and constructed by participants in Baltimore Open Works, our compact and lightwight WeatherCubes house ozone, nitrogen dioxide, temperture, and humidity. /p>

The Results

The cubes measure air quality and post their result once a day online, for everyone to access.

The Press

WeatherCubes and Baltimore Open Air have been featured in Baltimore Sun, JHU's The Hub, Baltimore Fish Bowl, and Technical.ly

The Point

Families and individuals can use the WeatherCube's information to make informed decisions about their heart and lung health.
